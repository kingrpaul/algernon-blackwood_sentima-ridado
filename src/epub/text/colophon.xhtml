“The Laughter of Courage” is a short story written by Algernon Blackwood, was first published in 1924 in Tongues of Fire and Other Sketches, and is believed to be in the public domain. Digital scans can be found at the Internet Archive. A transcription was produced by Paul King.

The work was translated from English to Esperanto as “Sentima Ridado” in 2023 by Paul King and that translation is released under the CC0 1.0 Universal (CC0 1.0) Public Domain Dedication.

The cover page was adapted from Safety Last! by Harold Lloyd, and is believed to be in the public domain.

The cover and title pages feature the League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by The League of Moveable Type.
